Lasagna

Description

Tender pasta sheets embrace velvety ricotta cheese, savory meat sauce, and a blanket of gooey mozzarella.

Ingredients‚Äù

  • 1 pound (450g) lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound (450g) ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28-ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 (6-ounce) can tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon sugar (optional, to balance acidity)
  • 15 ounces (425g) ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the Oven
  2. Cook the Lasagna Noodles
  3. Prepare the Meat Sauce
  4. Add Tomato Sauce
  5. Prepare the Ricotta Mixture
  6. Assemble the Lasagna
  7. Bake the Lasagna
  8. Let it Rest
  9. Serve
