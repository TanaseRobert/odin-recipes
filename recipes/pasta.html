Pasta

Description

Bursting with seasonal vegetables, al dente pasta, and a luscious garlic-infused sauce, every forkful is a celebration of freshness and taste.

Ingredients‚Äù

  • 12 ounces (340g) of your favorite pasta (such as spaghetti, fettuccine, or penne)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 medium carrot, julienned or thinly sliced
  • 1 small red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 small yellow bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup snap peas, trimmed
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup vegetable or chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream or half-and-half (optional for a creamier sauce)
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Red pepper flakes (optional, for added heat)
  • Fresh basil leaves, chopped, for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook the Pasta
  2. Prepare the Vegetables
  3. Add the Vegetables
  4. Create the Sauce
  5. Combine Pasta and Sauce
  6. Finish and Serve
  7. Serve Hot
